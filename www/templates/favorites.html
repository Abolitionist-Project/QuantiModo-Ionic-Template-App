Favorite Variables

Favorites are variables that you might want to track on a frequent but irregular basis. Examples might be as-needed medications or glasses of water.

Tip: I recommend using reminders instead of favorites whenever possible because they allow you to record regular 0 values as well. Having measurements indicating when you didn't take a medication or eat something for instance lead to much more accurate correctional analysis.
